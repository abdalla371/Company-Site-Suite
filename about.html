About Smart Employment

On a mission to transform employment in Somalia — connecting talent with opportunity.

Smart Employment Service is Somalia's leading employment platform dedicated to connecting job seekers, students, and graduates with the best employers across the country. Founded with the belief that every Somali deserves access to meaningful work, we bridge the gap between talent and opportunity through technology, programs, and community.

What We Offer

💼 Job Listings

Hundreds of verified job listings from top employers across Somalia — searchable by category, location, and type. Updated daily.

🎓 Internship Program

Our structured internship program places students and graduates with leading companies, complete with mentorship and a completion certificate.

💡 Shaqo-Tag Program

Short-term work attachments for professionals and job seekers looking to gain hands-on experience in a new sector.

🏢 HR Policy Program

We help companies build professional, fair HR policies — from job descriptions and interview processes to employee handbooks and performance management.
