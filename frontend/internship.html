Internship Program

Start your career with structured internships at leading Somali companies. Perfect for students and recent graduates.

About the Program

Our Internship Program connects university students and recent graduates with top companies across Somalia. Interns receive structured mentorship, hands-on experience, and a formal certificate upon completion. We match each intern with a company that fits their field of study and career goals.

Who Can Apply

  • University students in 2nd year or above
  • Recent graduates (within 2 years of graduation)
  • Any field of study — we have placements across all sectors
  • Somali nationals, both inside and outside Somalia

What You Get

  • 3–6 month structured internship placement
  • Mentorship from experienced industry professionals
  • Official completion certificate from Smart Employment
  • Networking access with top employers
  • Priority access to full-time job listings
  • Monthly allowance (varies by host company)

How It Works
