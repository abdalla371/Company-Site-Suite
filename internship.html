🎓 Internship Program

Gain real work experience, build professional skills, and jump-start your career in Somalia.

🌟 About the Program

Our Internship Program connects university students and recent graduates with top companies across Somalia. Interns receive structured mentorship, hands-on experience, and a formal certificate upon completion.

✅ Who Can Apply

  • University students in 2nd year or above
  • Recent graduates (within 2 years)
  • Any field of study — we have placements across sectors
  • Somali nationals living anywhere

🎁 What You Get

  • 3–6 month structured internship
  • Mentorship from industry professionals
  • Professional certificate from Smart Employment
  • Networking with top employers
  • Priority access to full-time job listings
  • Monthly allowance (varies by company)

📋 How It Works

1

Apply Online

Fill in the application form with your academic and personal details.

2

Review & Matching

Our team reviews your application and matches you with a suitable company.

3

Interview with the host company and confirm your placement.

4

Start Internship

Begin your internship with full support from Smart Employment.

5

Complete & Certify

Finish your internship and receive your official certificate.
