Shaqo-Tag Program

Work attachment program designed to give you practical, hands-on experience in your specific field of study.

What is Shaqo-Tag?

"Shaqo-Tag" means work attachment in Somali. This program places you directly inside a working organisation for 1–3 months, giving you the practical skills, professional network, and employer endorsement you need to advance your career.

Eligibility

  • Working professionals looking to switch sectors
  • Job seekers with limited formal experience
  • Graduates seeking their first job placement
  • Anyone looking to upgrade their practical skills

Available Sectors

  • Banking & Financial Services
  • Telecommunications & IT
  • Healthcare & Medicine
  • Education & Training
  • Logistics & Supply Chain
  • Government & Public Sector
  • Media & Communications
