💼 Shaqo-Tag Program

A short-term work attachment program giving you hands-on experience in your chosen sector.

💡 What is Shaqo-Tag?

"Shaqo-Tag" means work attachment in Somali. This program places you directly in a working environment for 1–3 months so you can develop practical skills, expand your network, and prove your abilities to employers.

✅ Eligibility

  • Any working professional looking to switch sectors
  • Job seekers with limited formal experience
  • Graduates seeking their first placement
  • Anyone looking to upgrade their practical skills

🏆 Available Sectors

  • Banking & Financial Services
  • Telecommunications & IT
  • Healthcare & Medicine
  • Education & Training
  • Logistics & Supply Chain
  • Government & Public Sector
  • Media & Communications
